# Stacks and Queues

First In Last Out - Stack

First In First Out - Queues

Actions of a stack
- push (add to top)
- pop (remove from top)
- peek (look at the top)

Actions of a Queue
- dequeue (remove from the front)
- enqueue (add to end)
